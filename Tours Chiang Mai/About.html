Adventure & Day Tours Chiang Mai have been offering travellers a variety of the best adventures on offer every day of the week. If you fly in on a Saturday and are wondering what to do tomorrow then dont hesitate and book yourself into something via our online booking page. Our staff are very experienced at organising the day so that everyone has a memorable time you will surely be bragging to all your friends about. Our online form will list windows of available pick up times conveniently from the door of your hotel. We encourage all our guests to join our Facebook group or Wechat Group straight away once booked this allows our staff to keep you informed right from the start by sending you an estimated time of arrival in the morning.

During filling out our online booking form once you have selected what adventour you would like to go on, a confirmation email will be sent detailing exactly what we will provide and what you should try and bring to have the most amount of fun.
